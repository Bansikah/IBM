Unit Conversions
Temperature




To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, use the formula replacing the 'c' with your temperature in celsius: (C x 9/5) + 32
Weight




To convert Kilograms to Pounds, use the formula "kg x 2.205"
Distance




To convert Kilograms to Pounds, use the formula "km / 1.609"
